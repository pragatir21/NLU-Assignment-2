The Health Centre (HC) has been started at the Permanent Campus of IIT Jodhpur from 15 June 2017. IIT Jodhpur provides round-the-clock health care facilities to Students, Faculty/ Staff Members and their dependents, also other residents and working staff of the Institute. This fully equipped and self-sufficient facility is run under the supervision of Professor-in-charge Health Center (PIC-HC) and Medical Officers (MO) with advice provided by the Medical Service User’s Committee (MSUC). Presently, the following facilities are available at the Health Centre (HC): In addition to on-campus healthcare, IIT Jodhpur has established formal agreements with several leading hospitals to ensure priority treatment for its students, faculty, staff, and their registered dependents. These include (1) Goyal Hospital and Research Centre, (2) MediPulse Hospital, (3) ASG Eye Hospital, (4) Vasundhara Hospital, and (5) AIIMS Jodhpur. The Institute Health Center (HC) plays a central role in coordinating and supervising the treatment of patients admitted to these empanelled hospitals. This ensures continuity of care and assistance during hospitalization. Arrangements are routinely made for specialist doctor visits on the campus (Psychiatrist, Gynaecologist, Paediatrician, ENT Specialists, Endocrinologist, Orthopedic Specialist) on a weekly basis. The Health Center also organizes various Health Awareness, Medical Screening, Vaccination Camps and Blood donation camps in coordination with Hospitals and other Agencies. The Health Center is the first point of contact for any medical needs, and it aims to cater & provide primary health care to the students, staff, faculties, and their dependent(s) in the campus. The following Institute Medical Officers are available on campus in rotating shifts, ensuring 24/7 medical coverage for the IITJ community: These officers are committed to addressing the general healthcare needs of students, staff, and faculty at any time of day. Sexual and reproductive health services, including education and counselling, are provided by the visiting Gynaecologists and Medical Officers. These services are essential to supporting the overall well-being and awareness of the campus community. One MD Physician is available daily (except Sundays) from 3:30 PM to 7:00 PM at the Health Center.
